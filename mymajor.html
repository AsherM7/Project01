Major Description

Cybersecurity involves safeguarding computer systems, networks, software, and information from unauthorized access and attacks. It encompasses developing defensive technologies for critical infrastructure and analyzing and countering cyber threats. This program focuses on analyzing cybersecurity data, identifying cyber incidents, understanding malware behavior and effectively communicating risks to business stakeholders and the public. Successful cyber analysts typically possess a solid foundation in mathematics and computation, often utilizing programming and scripting to address challenges and integrate tools. Skills involve analyzing data from intrusion detection systems, firewalls, and anti-malware software to protect systems and data.
