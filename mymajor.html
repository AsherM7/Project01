Major Description


Cybersecurity involves safeguarding computer systems, networks, software, and information from unauthorized access and attacks. Successful cyber analysts typically possess a solid foundation in mathematics and computation, often utilizing programming and scripting to address challenges and integrate tools. Skills involve analyzing data from intrusion detection systems, firewalls, and anti-malware software to protect systems and data.
