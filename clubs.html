Fundamentals of Computing Association

The FCA provides students with a platform to build and enhance technical skills desired by employers in the workforce through projects using Java, Python, cloud services, and more. In my role as Alumni Relations, I collaborate with alumni to cultivate and strengthen alumni community connections. As well as mobilized alumni networks to generate support for club objectives and provide mentorship.

IST Undergraduate Student Council

The IST undergraduate student council comprises elected executive members and acts as a representative body for all IST students. This council enables correspondence between students and the university administration, coordinates activities, and addresses concerns expressed by students. In my role as Outreach Director, I manage an active social media presence, which amplifies engagement and raises awareness.
